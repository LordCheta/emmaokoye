    • Born December 25, 1965 in Lagos, Nigeria. Prof Emma is a native of Nibo in Awka South Anambra State Nigeria. He is Married to Dr. Ebere Okoye with five lovely children.

    He is Specialized in pulic sector accounting, forensic accounting & auditing. Alongside being a professor of Accountancy, he also holds an MBA in Banking and Finance. He lectures at Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka.
