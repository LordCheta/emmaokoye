    • Born December 25, 1965 in Lagos, Nigeria. Prof Emma is a native of Nibo in Awka South Anambra State Nigeria. He is Married to Dr. Ebere Okoye with five lovely children.

    He is Specialized in pulic sector accounting, forensic accounting & auditing. Alongside being a professor of Accountancy, he also holds an MBA in Banking and Finance. He lectures at Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka.

    He has served in various administrative roles such as The HOD, department of accountancy, NAU. Sub-Dean and Dean Faculty of Management Sciences, NAU. Associate Director of Research, NAU BUsiness School. Director Nnamdi Azikiwe University Awka, Advisory Board of the Association of National Accountants of Nigeria (ANAN) Research Centre

    He has supervised over 200 MBA students, over 80 MSc Students and over 59 Ph.D students. He has over 300 journals and publications to his name and has served on several editoral boards.
